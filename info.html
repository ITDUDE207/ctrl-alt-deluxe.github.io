📋 Legend

🔑 Key - Found in the Hall (Required to win)
🧪 Potion - Found in the Kitchen (Required to win)
⭐ Goal - Reach the Garden with both items to win!

🎮 How to Play

Commands:

  • go north - Move north
  • go south - Move south
  • go east - Move east
  • go west - Move west
  • get key - Pick up the key
  • get potion - Pick up the potion

Winning Strategy:

  1. Start in the Hall
  2. Type get key to collect the key
  3. Type go south to move to the Kitchen
  4. Type get potion to collect the potion
  5. Type go east to reach the Garden
  6. You win! 🎉
